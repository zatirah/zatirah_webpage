MY FAMILY

This is My Family

My family consists of 8 members, and they are my source of happiness and strength. My eldest brother is married and now has an adorable child, making me a very proud aunt! In our family, only my younger sibling and I are still studying, and we work hard to achieve our dreams. My father and mother are the most resilient people, always supporting us and ensuring our family stays close-knit. Our bond is very strong, with each family member playing an important role, filling our lives with love, laughter, and beautiful memories together.

Big Family

This photo shows me with my mother’s side relatives. We frequently get together in large numbers, particularly around the holidays when those who are studying abroad come home. We make it a point to gather at home and share meals as a family because we don’t often go out together. When all of her kids and grandkids are at the house, my grandma is the happiest person alive, and it creates a cozy and vibrant environment that deepens our family ties.
