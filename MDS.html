Minggu Destini Siswa (MDS) Program

My MDS Experience

The MDS program was an exciting and enriching experience that welcomed all new students to the university. It was filled with various activities designed to help us adapt to university life and make lasting memories with our fellow students.

Highlights from the MDS Program:

  • Participated in many activities with fellow students.
  • Attended various informative sessions about university rules and resources.
  • Made new friends and connected with seniors and facilitators.
